Ring Gauges

A ring gauge, is a cylindrical ring of a thermally stable material, often steel, whose inside diameter is finished to gauge tolerance and is used for checking the external diameter of a cylindrical object. Ring gauges are used for comparative gauging as well as for checking, calibrating, or setting of gauges or other standards.

Spline Mandrels

Spline mandrels are essential accessories for machining and inspection operations like turning, milling gear cutting etc., for the production of splined components, where close relation must be maintained with internal spline profiles. Solid spline mandrels are manufactured to meet the requirement of various engineering industries.

Spline Broaches

A broach is a long multi-tooth cutting tool with successively deeper cuts. Broaching is used to machine internal and external surfaces, such as holes, keyways, and teeth of internal gears. The automotive industry relies on spline broaches to create transmission and driveshaft parts, among other items.
